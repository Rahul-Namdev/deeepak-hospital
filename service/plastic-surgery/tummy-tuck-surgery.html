TUMMY TUCK SURGERY

Tummy tuck surgery, or abdominoplasty, is a cosmetic procedure designed to remove excess skin and fat from the abdominal area while tightening the muscles of the abdominal wall. This surgery is particularly beneficial for individuals who have experienced significant weight loss or post pregnancy and are left with loose, sagging skin that is resistant to diet and exercise. At Deepak Hospital, expert team of Doctors and their team offer expert tummy tuck procedures to help patients achieve a firmer, flatter abdomen and improve their overall body contour.

Types of Tummy Tuck Procedures
  1. Full Tummy Tuck:This procedure is ideal for patients who need significant correction in both the upper and lower abdomen. It involves a horizontal incision between the hip bones, allowing for the removal of excess skin and fat and the tightening of abdominal muscles.
  2. Mini Tummy Tuck: Suitable for patients with less excess skin and fat, the mini tummy tuck focuses on the area below the navel. It involves a smaller incision and is less invasive than a full tummy tuck.
  3. Extended Tummy Tuck: For patients with excess skin extending to the flanks or lower back, an extended tummy tuck offers comprehensive contouring of the abdominal area and sides.
  4. Circumferential Tummy Tuck: Also known as a belt lipectomy, this procedure targets the entire midsection, including the abdomen, hips, and lower back, providing dramatic contouring results.
Treatment Process for Tummy Tuck Surgery
  • Consultation and Evaluation: Expert team of Doctors conducts a thorough consultation to assess the patient's goals and medical history. A personalized treatment plan is created to achieve the desired results.
  • Preoperative Preparation: Patients receive detailed instructions on how to prepare for surgery, including dietary guidelines, medication management, and lifestyle adjustments.
  • Surgical Procedure: Tummy tuck surgery is performed under general anesthesia. The chosen technique (full, mini, extended, or circumferential) dictates the incision size and placement. Excess skin and fat are removed, and the abdominal muscles are tightened.
  • Postoperative Care: After surgery, patients are provided with comprehensive aftercare instructions, including wound care, activity restrictions, and pain management.
  • Recovery and Rehabilitation: Recovery time varies depending on the extent of the surgery, but most patients can return to light activities within two weeks. Full recovery may take several months, during which patients are encouraged to follow a balanced diet and engage in gentle exercise.
  • Follow-up Appointments:Regular follow-up appointments with expert team of Doctors ensure that the healing process is on track and that any concerns are promptly addressed.
    • Benefits of Tummy Tuck Surgery
  • Enhanced Body Contour: Tummy tuck surgery provides a smoother, firmer abdominal profile, enhancing the overall body silhouette.
  • Improved Confidence: Patients often experience a boost in self-esteem and confidence following the procedure.
  • Correction of Diastasis Recti: The surgery effectively addresses the separation of abdominal muscles (diastasis recti) commonly caused by pregnancy.
  • Long-lasting Results: With a healthy lifestyle, the results of a tummy tuck can be long-lasting.
