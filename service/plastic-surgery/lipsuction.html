LIPOSUCTION

Liposuction is a popular cosmetic procedure designed to remove excess fat deposits and improve body contours. It is an effective solution for individuals struggling with stubborn fat that is resistant to diet and exercise. At Deepak Hospital, expert team of Doctors offers advanced liposuction techniques to help patients achieve their desired body shape with precision and care.

Understanding Liposuction

Liposuction involves the surgical removal of fat from specific areas of the body to enhance contour and proportions. It is not a weight-loss method but rather a body-sculpting procedure aimed at eliminating localized fat deposits that do not respond to traditional weight-loss methods.

Indications for Liposuction

Liposuction can be beneficial for individuals who:

  • Have stubborn fat deposits in areas such as the abdomen, thighs, hips, buttocks, arms, neck, or back..
  • Are close to their ideal body weight but desire more defined body contours..
  • Have good skin elasticity, allowing the skin to conform to new contours after fat removal.
  • Are in good overall health and have realistic expectations about the results.
Liposuction Techniques
  1. Tumescent Liposuction: This is the most common technique, where a saline solution mixed with anesthetic is injected into the target area. The solution helps to numb the area, reduce bleeding, and facilitate fat removal. A cannula is then inserted to suction out the fat
  2. Ultrasound-Assisted Liposuction (UAL): This technique uses ultrasound energy to liquefy fat cells, making them easier to remove. UAL is particularly effective for dense or fibrous areas such as the back or male breasts.
  3. Laser-Assisted Liposuction (LAL): LAL employs laser energy to liquefy fat cells before they are removed. It also stimulates collagen production, which can help tighten the skin in the treated area..
  4. Power-Assisted Liposuction (PAL): PAL involves the use of a vibrating cannula that breaks up fat cells, making them easier to extract. This method can reduce surgeon fatigue and improve precision.
