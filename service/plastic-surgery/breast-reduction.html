BREAST REDUCTION

Breast reduction, also known as reduction mammoplasty, is a surgical procedure designed to reduce the size and weight of large breasts, alleviating physical discomfort and improving body proportions. This surgery can provide significant relief from the physical and emotional burdens associated with excessively large breasts, such as back pain, neck pain, and skin irritation. At Deepak Hospital, expert team of Doctors specialized in breast reduction surgery, offering compassionate care and advanced surgical techniques to ensure optimal outcomes for patients.

Understanding Breast Reduction

Breast reduction involves removing excess breast tissue, fat, and skin to achieve a breast size in proportionate with the body. The procedure not only reduces breast size but also enhances breast shape and symmetry, improving overall aesthetics and quality of life.

Indications for Breast Reduction
  • Chronic Pain: Persistent back, neck, or shoulder pain caused by the weight of large breasts.
  • Skin Irritation:Rashes and skin irritation under the breast fold due to friction and moisture.
  • Posture Problems: Poor posture and discomfort resulting from the strain of carrying heavy breasts.
  • Activity Limitations: Restrictions on physical activities and exercise due to breast size
  • Self-Consciousness: Emotional distress or self-consciousness related to breast size and appearance.
Treatment Process for Breast Reduction
  1. Consultation and Evaluation: Expert team of Doctors conducts a thorough consultation and examination to understand the patient's concerns and goals. A personalized treatment plan is developed based on breast size, body proportions, and desired outcomes.
  2. Preoperative Preparation: Patients receive detailed instructions on preparing for surgery, including dietary guidelines and lifestyle adjustments. Medical evaluations may be conducted to ensure overall health and readiness for surgery.
  3. Surgical Procedure:Breast reduction surgery is typically performed under general anesthesia. Expert team of Doctors remove excess breast tissue, fat, and skin, and reshape the remaining tissue to create a more proportionate breast size. The incisions are then closed, and the nipples are repositioned for natural appearance.
  4. Postoperative Care: After surgery, patients are provided with comprehensive aftercare instructions, including wound care and pain management. A supportive surgical bra is recommended to aid in healing and provide comfort..
  5. Recovery and Rehabilitation: Recovery time varies, but most patients can resume light activities within two weeks. Full recovery may take several weeks, during which patients are advised to avoid strenuous activities and follow a healthy lifestyle.
  6. Follow-up Appointments: Regular follow-up appointments with expert team of Doctors ensure that the healing process is progressing smoothly and that any concerns are promptly addressed.
Benefits of Breast Reduction Surgery
  • Physical Relief: Relieving of back, neck, and shoulder pain, as well as skin irritation
  • Improved Posture: Enhance the posture and reduced the strain on the body due to lighter breast weight.
  • Restored Volume:The procedure effectively restores breast volume lost due to aging, pregnancy, or weight fluctuations
  • Greater Mobility:: Increased ability to engage in physical activities and exercise without discomfort.
  • Enhanced Self-Confidence: Improved body image and self-esteem, resulting in a more positive outlook.
  • Balanced Proportions: A more proportionate and aesthetically pleasing breast size in harmony with body contours.
