SPINE SURGERY

Spine surgery encompasses a variety of procedures aimed at addressing issues related to the spinal column, often focusing on alleviating pain, correcting deformities, or restoring function. At Deepak Hospital, Dr. Bhaskar Shetty and his team are dedicated to providing state-of-the-art spine care with a focus on personalized treatment plans to meet each patient's unique needs.

What is Spine Surgery?

Spine surgery involves surgical intervention to treat disorders of the spine, including herniated discs, spinal stenosis, scoliosis, and other degenerative conditions. The goal of spine surgery is to relieve pain, stabilize the spine, and improve overall function and quality of life

Types of Spine Surgery
  1. Discectomy::Removal of a portion of a herniated disc that is pressing on a nerve root or the spinal cord, relieving pain and other symptoms.
  2. Laminectomy: Removal of the lamina, the back part of the vertebra, to create more space within the spinal canal and to relive pressure on the spinal cord and nerves..
  3. Spinal Fusion: Joining two or more vertebrae to eliminate motion between them, often used to treat instability or deformity of the spine..
  4. Foraminotomy: Enlargement of the foramen, the space through which nerve roots exit the spine, to relieve pressure on compressed nerves.
  5. Artificial Disc Replacement: Replacing a damaged spinal disc with an artificial one to maintain normal spine hieght and movement of the spine and to relive the pain.
  6. Kyphoplasty/Vertebroplasty: Minimally invasive procedures to treat the vertebral compression fractures by stabilizing the fracture with bone cement.
Benefits of Spine Surgery
  • Pain Relief: Effective in reliveing acute /chronic pain caused by nerve compression or spinal instability.
  • Improved Mobility: Restores movement and flexibility by removing or repairing damaged structures.
  • Enhanced Quality of Life: Reduces pain and improves physical function, allowing patients to return to daily activities and to achive a better quality of life.
  • Correction of Deformities: Addresses structural abnormalities such as scoliosis or kyphosis.
    • Treatment and Recovery Process
  • Consultation and Evaluation:Dr. Bhaskar Shetty conducts a comprehensive evaluation, including physical exams, medical history, and diagnostic imaging (MRI, CT scans, X-rays) to assess the condition and determine the most appropriate treatment.
  • Surgical Procedure: Depending on the condition, spine surgery is performed under general anesthesia, with duration varying based on the complexity of the procedure.
  • Postoperative Care: Patients will get thorough postoperative care, including pain management, wound care, and rehabilation protocol.
  • Correction of Deformities: A tailored rehabilitation program is essential for recovery, focusing on exercises to strengthen the back muscles, improve flexibility, and promote healing.
  • Follow-up Appointments: Regular follow-up visits with Dr. Shetty are scheduled to monitor progress, address any concerns, and adjust treatment plans as necessary.
