SPORTS INJURIES

All About Sports Injuries and Their Treatments

Sports injuries are common among athletes and active individuals. Proper treatment and rehabilitation are crucial for recovery. At Deepak Hospital, Dr. Bhaskar Shetty and Dr. Anvith Shetty specialied in sports Injuries and Rehabilation.

Common Sports Injuries
  1. Sprains and Strains: Ligament injuries, typically in the ankle, knee, or wrist.
  2. Fractures:Broken bones from direct blows or twisting, common in the wrist, arm, and leg.
  3. Dislocations:Joints Displaced out of position may Affect knee, Hip, Shoulder, Ankle and finger joints.
  4. Tendinitis:Inflammation of the tendon affecting the tendons of Achilles, elbow, or shoulder Joints.
  5. Meniscus Tears:Knee cartilage tear is common in footballers, Cricketers and basketball Players.
  6. ACL Injuries:Torn anterior cruciate ligament injury is common in footballers, Cricketers and basketball Players.
Treatment Options

1. Non-Surgical:

  • RICE (Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation): Reduces pain and swelling.
  • Medications: Anti-inflammatory drugs for pain and inflammation.
  • Physical Therapy: Restores strength, flexibility, and range of motion.
  • Follow-Up Care: Bracing: Supports and stabilizes during recovery..

2. Surgical:

  • Arthroscopy: Minimally invasive joint surgery
  • Reconstructive Surgery: Repairing of ligaments, meniscus, labral tear and ACL tears.
  • Tendon Repair: Reconstruction of torn ligaments and tendons.
Recovery and Rehabilitation Personalized programs /include:
  • Strengthening Exercises: Rebuild muscle strength.
  • Flexibility Training: Restores range of motion.
  • Balance Exercises: Improves stability.
  • Gradual Return to Activity: Safely returns to sports.
