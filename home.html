About Us

Deepak Orthopaedic and General Hospital, established in 1996, is a premier healthcare facility located in the Western Suburbs of Mumbai, specifically in Mira Road. With over 25 years of dedicated service, Deepak Hospital has been a trusted provider of medical care for the communities of Mira Road and Bhayander

The hospital features 25 inpatient beds equipped with advanced technology, making it a leading center for trauma and joint replacement. Specializing in complex trauma, fractures, head injuries, and general medical cases, Deepak Hospital offers high-quality care at affordable prices.

Mission

To deliver exceptional care across all multispecialties and specialized sports-related treatments using the latest technology, cutting-edge research, high-quality standards, and cost-effective services.

Vision

To provide state-of-the-art surgeries across various multispecialty departments, ensuring top-quality rehabilitation, prevention, and treatment of health issues and injuries. Our goal is to achieve the best possible outcomes, enabling each patient to enjoy an active and pain-free lifestyle after treatment or surgery. We stay abreast of the latest developments by continually updating the knowledge of our medical and paramedical personnel.

Our hospital boasts a dedicated team of doctors, therapists, and technicians available 24/7, led by Dr. Bhaskar Shetty. We focus on all specialties in joint-related ailments of the shoulder, knee, and hip, treating conditions such as knee arthritis, knee ligament tears, stiff shoulders, and shoulder instability.
